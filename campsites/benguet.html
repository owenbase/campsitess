Valleypoint Benguet

Valleypoint is more than a destination—it's an experience that blends the charm of the mountains with an array of offerings to make your stay truly unforgettable. The serene ambiance of the surroundings sets the stage for a retreat that seamlessly combines relaxation and adventure.

Begin your Valleypoint journey with a dining experience like no other. The on-site restaurant invites you to savor delectable meals while surrounded by the natural beauty of Benguet. Whether it's a cozy meal for two or a group celebration, Valleypoint's dining experience is a feast for the senses.

For those seeking a unique stay, Valleypoint introduces the allure of glamping—where glamour meets camping. Picture yourself nestled in cozy tents, surrounded by the crisp mountain air and the soothing sounds of nature. Glamping at Valleypoint is not just an accommodation; it's a chance to connect with the great outdoors without sacrificing comfort.

Shared room accommodations offer another delightful option, providing a communal space for camaraderie and shared moments. Wake up to panoramic views of the mountain valleys, creating a sense of serenity that lingers long after your stay.
