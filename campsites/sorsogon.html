The Last Campsite by Modern Hippies

Welcome to The Last Campsite, where solidarity and secrets to happiness is whispered through humid tents, swaying hammocks, and bonfires. Who would have though that a 10-minute terrain would give you an incomparable level of peace and comfort, surrounded by nothing but trees and great people. Mornings are the best here in The Last Campsite, you will be woken up by the most ancient melodies sung by birds and the scent of damp grass as you drink your coffee.

It’s a complete set-up perfect for campers, from hiking, to grilling and bonfires, and a mini-bar for those who want to chill under the night sky. It’s like a home away from home, the entire crew is hospitable beyond compare treating their visitors like one of them, just like family. Of course camping wouldn’t be complete without a quick side trip, Nagsipit Falls can be found through a trail of rich biodiversity that stretches into an endless deep, untamed vegetation. The falls itself is small but picturesque, enveloped by a canopy of the gentle rustling of trees.

Go and visit The Last Campsite and have a taste of complete emotional bliss. See you there!
