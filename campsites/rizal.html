Viewscape Nature's Park, Tanay Rizal

Viewscape Natureâ€™s Park is a new camping area and nature park located in Sitio Maysawa Barangay Cuyambay Tanay Rizal, which is a few hours away from the city. They offer affordable lodging options such as camping, glamping, car camping, and private rooms.

This place has one of the most scenic mountain views, but the best part is the sea of clouds during sunrise and the beautiful and clear night sky. Which is something that most people who want to unwind and reconnect with nature would enjoy.

They offer a variety of activities for adrenaline junkies, such as Caldera Bath, Astrophotography, and the Adventure Trail experience, which includes exploring campgrounds, visiting caves, and bird watching.

As a lover of the night sky, I thoroughly enjoyed stargazing and astrophotography here. For me, the starry night sky is a source of peace and healing.
