Camp Puor Pangasinan

Welcome to a sanctuary where the echoes of nature's symphony blend seamlessly with the warmth of authentic Filipino hospitality—welcome to your everlasting camping experience at our haven.

As you traverse the grounds, you'll discover that our camp is designed to seamlessly integrate with the natural beauty that surrounds it. Cozy camping spots are tucked away amidst the foliage, providing a retreat for those seeking solitude, while communal areas invite shared stories and laughter—a perfect balance between the tranquility of nature and the warmth of human connection.

Our commitment to authentic Filipino hospitality extends to every corner of the camp. From the moment you arrive, you'll be embraced by the genuine warmth of our staff, who are not just caretakers but storytellers eager to share the tales of the land. The aroma of traditional Filipino dishes wafting through the air promises a culinary journey that mirrors the richness of our culture.

At our camp, we invite you to partake in an everlasting camping experience—a tapestry of adventure, tranquility, and Filipino hospitality that lingers in the heart long after you bid the outdoors farewell. Whether you seek the thrill of nature, the embrace of community, or a taste of authentic Filipino life, our camp is your canvas for a truly immersive and everlasting camping experience.
