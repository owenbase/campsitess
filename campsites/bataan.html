Tarak Ridge Campsite Bataan

Embark on an adventure to the enchanting Tarak Ridge in Mariveles, Bataan, where nature's grandeur unveils itself in a breathtaking panorama. Perched at an elevation of 1,006 meters above sea level, this majestic ridge offers a picturesque view that extends far and wide, capturing the beauty of Manila Bay, the historic Corregidor Island, and glimpses of Manila and Cavite.

As you ascend towards the Tarak Peak, reaching a height of 1,130 meters above sea level, you'll find yourself immersed in the awe-inspiring beauty of the surroundings. The journey to Tarak Ridge is not just a trek; it's a symphony of natural wonders waiting to be explored.
