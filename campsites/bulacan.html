Camp Kazoku (Camp Omada) Bulacan

Step into a world of warmth, camaraderie, and the spirit of family as Camp Kazoku, formerly known as Camp Omada, extends its arms wide open to welcome you. It is with great joy and anticipation that we announce the reopening of this cherished haven, now ready and open for reservations.

BCamp Kazoku is not just a place; it's an invitation to a community bound by shared memories, laughter, and the joy of being together. Formerly known as Camp Omada, this beloved retreat has undergone a transformation, but its essence remains true—the spirit of family and togetherness.

The name "Kazoku" itself, meaning "family" in Japanese, embodies the heart and soul of this camp. It's a celebration of connections, relationships, and the shared experiences that form the fabric of a family—whether by blood or by choice.

As the doors of Camp Kazoku swing open once again, we invite you to be a part of this journey. Imagine a place where the air is filled with the sounds of joyous reunions, where the laughter of children mingles with the rustle of leaves, and where the bonds of family are strengthened by the embrace of nature.
